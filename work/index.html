This is what I do.

I've always been a wearer of many hats.

It keeps me on my toes.

Here are some of the areas I work in besides web design.

Italian>English Translation

I've been translating professionally for over ten years.
My specializations include art history and criticsm, travel and tourism, history, and marketing.
If you have an Italian text you need translated into English, please feel free to contact me with the project details and I'll be in touch with a quote.

EFL Instruction

My first experience as a tutor dates back to when I was seventeen (unless playing teacher counts).
I earned my Certificate in Teaching English as a Foreign Language in 2005 and have taught in schools, companies, even the military.
My current focus is English for Special Purposes in business and academic settings.

Teacher Training

In early 2017 I developed a week-long intensive training course for school staff called Mindfulness for Teachers, and since then I've successfully run it at the Europass Teacher Training Academy.
In addition to teaching my own course, I've taught CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning) and Classroom Management.
